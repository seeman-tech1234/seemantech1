Nawab Shah Alam Khan College of Education is located at Yennepally, Vikarabad Mandal & District of Telangana. The college has established in the academic year 2007-2008. The college is affiliated Osmania University, recognize by National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) and permitted by government of Telangana for the two years B.Ed course. The college campus is very peaceful and free of all sorts of pollutions. The beautiful clean and green campus covers on 7.2 Acres of land with two stories building having a built-up area of 3080 sq.mt. The college is a co-educational and self-financed minority institute with the two basic unites of students thus the institution every year accommodate 100 new students in B.Ed course and it has 200 total enrolled students of both years..


The college admits qualified and punctual students; the students should be presented in all the classes during the working days. The students should have 80% attendance in theory classes and complete all the assessments, records and practical attendance should be 90% for writing university semester examinations. The college is free from ragging, not a signal care reported in last 15 years; there is an anti-ragging committee headed by principal to monitor such acts.
